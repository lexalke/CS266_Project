THE VOY BAND

ABOUT

The VOY Band originated in 1989 with six members, Gary "The Frog" Swanson and Ray Schnarr. With the "Frog" on keyboards and Schnarr on guitar and vocals, they needed some rhythm, they added "The Great Piersono" for drums and laughs. As time has progressed the band has evolved into a multimusical extravaganza covering music from the forties to the nineties spread over a vast range of genres from country to pop rock.
